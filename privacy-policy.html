2. Data We Collect

2.1 Breathing Session Data (Stored Locally)

When you use Deep Flow, we collect and store the following data only on your device:

  • Session Duration: How long each breathing session lasted
  • Breathing Pattern Used: Which pattern you selected (e.g., "Calm", "Focus", "SOS Calm")
  • Mood Data: Your mood before and after each session (optional)
  • Triggers: What prompted you to use the app (optional)
  • Session Notes: Any personal notes you add (optional)
  • Cycles Completed: Number of breathing cycles you completed
  • Timestamp: When the session ended
  • Session History: All past sessions visible in your "Stats" screen
💡 Key Point: All this data is stored on your phone using SharedPreferences (local storage). We do NOT upload this data to any server.

2.2 Advertising Data

  • Google AdMob AD_ID: When you first open the app, Google's Mobile Ads SDK collects your device's advertising ID for ad serving
  • Ad Impressions & Clicks: Google AdMob tracks which ads you see and interact with

For details on how Google processes this data, see Google's Privacy Policy

2.3 Notification Data

When you enable Daily Reminders:

  • Reminder Schedule: Your preferred time for daily breathing reminders
  • Reminder Status: Whether you have reminders enabled or disabled

Notification data is processed locally on your device only. We do NOT track whether you tapped, dismissed, or ignored reminders.

2.4 App Settings & Preferences

  • Theme Preference: Light/Dark mode, dynamic colors
  • Sound Settings: Whether sounds are enabled
  • Haptic Feedback: Whether vibrations are enabled
  • Notification Preferences: Which types of notifications you want
  • Pro Status: Whether you're using Free or Pro version

All stored locally on your device.

2.5 In-App Purchase Data

If you purchase the Pro version:

  • Transaction ID: Unique identifier for your purchase
  • Purchase Date & Time: When you made the purchase
  • Purchase Amount: How much you paid
  • Payment Method: Processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store

This data is managed by Apple (for iOS) or Google Play (for Android). We do NOT store payment details directly.

2.6 Analytics & Crash Reporting

✓ Privacy-First Approach:
  • Deep Flow does NOT use Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics
  • We do NOT collect crash logs or usage analytics
  • Your app usage is completely private to you

4. How We Use Your Data

4.1 Session Data (Local Use)

Your breathing session data is used to:

  • Display your statistics in the Stats screen
  • Calculate streaks and milestones
  • Track mood trends to help you see if breathing techniques are helping
  • Generate session reports that you can export for your personal records
  • Improve your experience by remembering your favorite patterns

4.2 Advertising Data

Your AD_ID is used to:

  • Show relevant ads from Google's ad network
  • Personalize ads based on your interests (you can opt out in device settings)
  • Measure ad performance to improve the app's monetization
💡 You have control: You can opt out of personalized ads in your Android/iOS settings without losing app functionality.

4.3 Notification Data

Your reminder preferences are used to:

  • Send you daily breathing reminders at your preferred time
  • Respect your preferences (no reminders if you disable them)

5. Data Retention & Deletion

5.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Session History: Stored indefinitely until you manually delete
  • Settings & Preferences: Stored until you uninstall the app
  • Notifications: Stored until you change your reminder settings

5.2 How to Delete Your Data

You can delete all Deep Flow data by:

In-App Method:

  1. Go to Settings → Stats → "Clear All Data" button
  2. This deletes all session history, mood tracking, and personal notes

Device Settings Method:

  • Android: Settings → Apps → Deep Flow → Storage → Clear Cache/Clear Storage
  • iOS: Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Deep Flow → Offload App or Delete App

Complete Removal:

  • Simply uninstall the app
  • All data stored on your device is immediately deleted
  • Your purchase (Pro) may persist in your App Store account (managed by Apple/Google)
⚠️ Important: Once deleted, data cannot be recovered. We do not maintain backups of your personal session data.

6. Data Security

6.1 How We Protect Your Data

  • Local Storage: All session data is encrypted by your device's OS (Android uses encryption, iOS uses Secure Enclave)
  • No Server Transmission: Your breathing data never leaves your phone
  • Permissions-Based: We only request permissions we actually use (Vibrate, Notifications, Alarms)
  • No Persistent IDs: We do not create user accounts or IDs

6.2 What Could Go Wrong?

While we take security seriously, risks include:

  • Device theft: Someone with access to your unlocked phone could view your data
  • OS vulnerabilities: Android/iOS bugs could theoretically expose data
  • Malware: Malicious software on your phone could access app data
Mitigation:
  • Use device lock (PIN, password, biometric)
  • Keep your OS updated
  • Install apps only from official stores
