1. Overview

DeepFlow is a premium breathing wellness app designed to help you practice mindfulness, reduce stress, and improve your mental well-being through guided breathing exercises.

🔒 Our Privacy Promise

DeepFlow operates on a privacy-first, offline-only model. Your personal breathing data, mood logs, and session history are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to external servers. We do not collect, track, or sell your personal information.

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer

DeepFlow is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition. It is designed as a wellness and educational tool. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice.

2. Data We Collect

All data listed below is stored locally on your device only using encrypted local storage (SharedPreferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS).

2.1 Breathing Session Data

  • Session Metadata: Duration, pattern used, cycles completed, timestamp
  • Mood Tracking: Optional pre/post session mood selection
  • Triggers & Notes: Optional context about why you practiced
  • Progress Metrics: Streaks, total minutes, pattern preferences

2.2 App Preferences

  • Visual Settings: Theme mode, color theme, orb selection
  • Audio Settings: Sound on/off, volume levels, cue style
  • Haptic Settings: Vibration preferences
  • Notification Settings: Reminder schedules and preferences

2.3 Purchase Information

If you purchase DeepFlow Pro (one-time lifetime unlock):

  • Transaction is processed securely by Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS)
  • We receive only a purchase verification token — no payment details, card numbers, or personal financial information
  • Your Pro status is stored locally and can be restored via the store's receipt system
✅ Zero Analytics • Zero Tracking

DeepFlow does not use Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Google Analytics, or any third-party tracking SDKs. We do not collect crash reports, usage statistics, or behavioral data. Your app experience is completely private.

4. How We Use Your Data

4.1 Local-Only Processing

Your session data is used exclusively on your device to:

  • Display your personal statistics and progress in the Stats screen
  • Calculate streaks, milestones, and practice insights
  • Generate optional PDF wellness reports for your personal records
  • Remember your favorite patterns and settings for a personalized experience
  • Provide mood trend analysis to help you observe your well-being journey

4.2 Notification System

If you enable Daily Reminders:

  • Your preferred reminder time is stored locally
  • Notifications are scheduled using your device's native notification system
  • We do not track whether you view, dismiss, or act on reminders

4.3 No External Processing

Important: DeepFlow has no backend server, no cloud sync, and no data transmission. All processing happens locally on your device. This architecture ensures your privacy by design.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

5.1 Retention Policy

  • Session History: Stored indefinitely until you choose to delete
  • Preferences: Stored until app uninstall or manual reset
  • Pro Purchase: Managed by Google Play/Apple App Store; can be restored anytime

5.2 How to Delete Your Data

🗑️ In-App Deletion

  1. Open DeepFlow → Settings → Stats
  2. Tap "Clear All Data"
  3. Confirm deletion

This permanently deletes all session history, mood data, and personal notes.

📱 Device-Level Deletion

  • Android: Settings → Apps → DeepFlow → Storage → Clear Data
  • iOS: Settings → General → iPhone Storage → DeepFlow → Delete App

🔄 Complete Removal

Uninstalling the app immediately deletes all locally stored data. Your Pro purchase status can be restored via your store account if you reinstall.

⚠️ Irreversible Action

Deleted data cannot be recovered. DeepFlow does not maintain backups of your personal session data. Please export any important reports before deletion.

6. Data Security

6.1 Protection Measures

  • Local Encryption: Your device's OS encrypts app data (Android File-Based Encryption, iOS Data Protection)
  • No Network Transmission: Session data never leaves your device
  • Minimal Permissions: We only request permissions essential for core functionality
  • No User Accounts: No login, no email, no persistent identifiers

6.2 Potential Risks & Mitigation

Risk Scenario Mitigation
Device theft or loss Use device lock (PIN, password, biometric). Data is encrypted at rest.
OS vulnerability Keep your operating system updated with latest security patches.
Malware on device Install apps only from official stores; use reputable security software.
Physical access to unlocked device Enable auto-lock; use app-level privacy features if needed.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 Global Rights Summary

  • Right to Access: View all your stored data via the Stats screen

  • Other Jurisdictions: We apply privacy-by-default principles globally.
