Privacy Policy

🔒 100% Offline

Deep Flow works completely offline. No internet connection is required to use any feature of this app. Your breathing sessions, statistics, and preferences are never transmitted to any server.

📦 Data Stored Locally

All your data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Session history and statistics
  • Personal preferences and settings
  • Custom breathing patterns
  • Reminder schedules

We do not have access to any of this data.

😊 Mood Tracking (Optional)

You can optionally log your mood before and after sessions. This data helps you understand which breathing patterns work best for you. All mood data stays on your device.

🚫 Advertising

Deep Flow is completely ad-free. We do not display any advertisements and do not use any ad networks. Your breathing sessions are never interrupted.

Our only revenue comes from a one-time Pro purchase. No subscriptions, no ads, no hidden fees.

🔔 Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notification data is processed locally on your device. We do not collect or track notification interactions.

🗑️ Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.

👶 Children's Privacy

This app is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

⚠️ Health Disclaimer

Deep Flow is a wellness app designed for relaxation and breath awareness. It is NOT a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or cure any medical condition. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
